Traffic squad management system

Traffic Squad Management System is a web site that helps the traffic police as well as the police by means of time and efficiency. The increasing importance of corruption has become a major factor. The existing system makes the use of pen and paper which is a challan that is given to the offender on breaking the traffic rules. As the system consists of paperwork the papers mostly get damaged or tempered. The proposed web application is provided to traffic police to create an online challan with the vehicle owner and vehicle photo as proof, user side receives the challan receipt with fine details.Traffic Management Systems (TMS) provide permanent control across the network, automatically sets routes for trains and logs train movements as well as detects and solves potential conflicts. Road Traffic Control Systems collect and analyze driving information of vehicles in the area, perform the optimal traffic signal control in accordance with the constantly changing road traffic situation, and provide useful traffic information to drivers based on the data collected.


In challan section, user can view his/her challan and also pay the challan fine amount which is imposed by traffic police officer.
